Pink Banana Storm: Bounce, Catch, and Score!

Enter the delightful world of Pink Banana Storm, where a charming pink girl invites you to her fruit garden and candy factory.

Key Features:

🍌 Charming Pink Theme: Enjoy beautiful animations in a whimsical pink setting.

🎀 Interactive Gameplay: Choose a ball and use the platform to bounce it left and right.
